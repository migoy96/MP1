Salud PH: Your Premier Online Booze Shop in the Philippines


Welcome to Salud PH, your ultimate destination for premium beverages and an exquisite collection of craft brews and spirits. As the foremost online booze shop situated in the heart of Manila, Philippines, we take pride in connecting you with the finest creations from newly established local breweries and distilleries.


Our Story:


Salud PH was born out of a shared passion for exceptional libations and the desire to celebrate the burgeoning craft beverage scene in the Philippines. Established as a response to the growing demand for unique, high-quality alcoholic beverages, our platform bridges the gap between discerning consumers and the innovative minds behind local breweries and distilleries.

Our Mission:


Our mission is two-fold: to bring the distinct flavors of Philippines newly established breweries directly to your doorstep, and to foster a community of beer enthusiasts who appreciate the artistry behind every brew. We are committed to providing an unparalleled online shopping experience that seamlessly combines convenience with a curated selection of unique beers, all while supporting the growth of local businesses.


Why Choose Us?

Curated Selection:

We carefully curate our offerings to ensure that each beer we feature tells a story and provides a one-of-a-kind tasting experience. From crisp lagers to bold IPAs and rich stouts, we have something for every palate.



Supporting Local:

We are proud to champion local brewers and contribute to the growth of the craft beer industry in the Philippines. With every purchase, you're not just enjoying a fantastic brew - you're supporting the dreams and aspirations of passionate local artisans.

Convenience:

Why brave the traffic when you can have your favorite beers delivered right to your doorstep? Our user-friendly online platform makes browsing, selecting, and ordering your preferred beverages a breeze.



Exceptional Service:

Your satisfaction is our priority. Our team is dedicated to ensuring that your shopping experience is smooth and enjoyable. Have a question? Need a recommendation? We're here to assist you every step of the way.
